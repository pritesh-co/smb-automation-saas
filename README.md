# smb-automation-saas
An SMB automation SaaS is a cloud-based software that eliminates repetitive, manual tasks by streamlining workflows. It replaces spreadsheets and disjointed processes with intelligent systems, helping small teams scale their operations, reduce operational costs, and boost productivity without requiring dedicated IT support.

An AI SaaS product where any Indian business owner types what they want to automate in plain English or Hindi — and the product builds the automation for them automatically. No technical knowledge needed.
